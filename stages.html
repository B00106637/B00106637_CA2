Stages

Main Stage

The Main Stage will play host to some of the world's best indie, alternative and leftfield acts that will provide the perfect backdrop to Electric Picnic.



Electric Arena

Just moments away from the Main Stage sits the Electric Arena: expect the unexpected. This tent has played host to some truly unforgettable performances over the years from artists spanning everything from electronica to indie and rock.

Rankin's Wood

Experience an eclectic mix of artists at the Rankin's Wood stage. The stage is named after the woods that cover Stradbally Estate.
